ABOUT US

Our goal

The Olympiad is designed to inspire 9th and 10th grade students to engage with learning in a meaningful and exciting way. By solving creative, interdisciplinary challenges in subjects such as STEM, economics, and history, students are encouraged to think beyond standard school material and explore real-world connections between disciplines.

Working in pairs, participants develop not only their academic knowledge but also essential skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration. The Olympiad highlights how different fields intersect and how they can be applied in future studies and careers. Our mission is to foster intellectual curiosity, empower young people to discover their potential, and create an environment where learning is both challenging and rewarding.

Our project presented at United Nations High Level Political Forum in New York this summer.
