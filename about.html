Who we are

We are a passionate team of students and educators from Riga State Gymnasium No.1, dedicated to creating a dynamic interdisciplinary Olympiad that brings together fields like physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, and more. Our goal is to foster an environment of creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration for 9th and 10th grade students.
